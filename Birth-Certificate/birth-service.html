Need Help with Birth Services?

Welcome to the Civil Registry Information System of the City of Manila! This platform is dedicated to serving residents and individuals with civil registration concerns within Manila.

Here’s what you can do under Birth Services in the City of Manila:

  • New Birth Registration:
    - Register a newly born child within Manila.
    - Ensure the birth is officially recorded in the city’s civil registry.
  • Request a Birth Certificate:
    - Obtain a Certified True Copy of a birth certificate issued in Manila for personal, legal, or official purposes.
  • Late Birth Registration:
    - Register births that were not filed within the standard 30-day reporting period, as mandated by the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA).
    - Applies to children born in Manila but registered beyond the normal timeframe.
  • Delayed Registration of Birth (Beyond 30 Days):
    - For individuals of any age who were born in Manila but whose birth has never been registered.
    - Requires supporting documents such as school records, baptismal certificates, or affidavits.
    - The system will guide you through each requirement specific to Manila’s local civil registry office.
  • Correction of Entries:
    - Correct clerical or typographical errors on existing birth records issued in Manila.
    - Submit documentary requirements and track your request online.

These services are available only for births that occurred in the City of Manila. All applications are processed in accordance with local regulations and PSA guidelines.

You may initiate your requests online, and receive updates through SMS or email. For in-person follow-ups or submission of original documents, please proceed to the Manila Civil Registry Office.

Need additional assistance? Email us at support@manilacivil.gov.ph or visit the office during business hours.

We are committed to providing fast, accurate, and citizen-friendly services to everyone in Manila. Thank you for using our system.
