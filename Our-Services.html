Service Guide

  • Select a Service
    - Choose from Birth, Marriage, or Death Services by clicking the Start Now button under each category.
  • Choose a Request Type
    - Each service offers multiple request types (e.g., Schedule of Appointment, corrections, late registration). Select the one that fits your need.
  • Fill Out the Form
    - Provide the necessary details step by step. Ensure all information is accurate before proceeding.
  • Submit & Track
    - Once submitted, you’ll receive a reference number to track your request status or make updates later.

You may submit requests online and receive status updates via SMS or email. For submissions requiring original documents or personal verification, please proceed to the Manila Civil Registry Office.

Need additional assistance? Email us at support@manilacivil.gov.ph or visit our office during business hours.

We are committed to delivering accurate, efficient, and citizen-friendly services. Thank you for using our system.
