Need Help?

Welcome to the Civil Registry Information System of the City of Manila! This platform is designed to provide residents and individuals with fast, secure, and convenient access to civil registration services.

Below is a summary of the services available through our system:

  • Birth Services:
    - Register a newly born child within the City of Manila.
    - Request a Certified True Copy of a birth certificate.
    - Submit late or delayed birth registrations.
    - Request corrections or annotations to birth records.
  • Marriage Services:
    - Apply for and register a marriage license.
    - Register your marriage to obtain an official certificate.
    - Request a Certified True Copy of a marriage certificate (with home delivery).
    - Submit delayed registrations or corrections to marriage records.
  • Death Services:
    - Officially register a death and obtain a legal death certificate.
    - Request a Certified True Copy of a death certificate (with home delivery).
    - Submit delayed registrations or corrections to death records.
    - Apply for burial or cremation permits.

You may submit requests online and receive status updates via SMS or email. For submissions requiring original documents or personal verification, please proceed to the Manila Civil Registry Office.

Need additional assistance? Email us at support@manilacivil.gov.ph or visit our office during business hours.

We are committed to delivering accurate, efficient, and citizen-friendly services. Thank you for using our system.
