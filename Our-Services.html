Need Help?

Welcome to the Civil Registry Information System of the City of Manila! This platform was designed to make your civil registration needs faster, easier, and more accessible.

Here’s a brief guide to help you navigate our services:

  • Birth Services:
    - Register a newly born child.
    - Request a Certified True Copy of a birth certificate.
    - Submit late birth registration or correction of entries.
  • Marriage Services:
    - Apply for a marriage license.
    - Register your marriage certificate.
    - Request a Certified True Copy of a marriage certificate.
    - Submit a request for correction or annotation.
  • Death Services:
    - Register a death and issue a death certificate.
    - Request a Certified True Copy of a death certificate.
    - Correct information on an existing death record.

Our goal is to provide you with a secure and streamlined experience. You can initiate your requests online and, where applicable, receive updates via SMS or email regarding your document’s status and availability.

Need additional support? Don’t hesitate to contact us via email at support@manilacivil.gov.ph or visit your nearest Civil Registry office for in-person assistance.
