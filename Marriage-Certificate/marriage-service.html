Need Help with Marriage Services?

Welcome to the Civil Registry Information System of the City of Manila! This platform is dedicated to assisting residents and individuals with marriage-related civil registration services within Manila.

Here’s what you can do under Marriage Services in the City of Manila:

  • Schedule of Appointment:
    - Book your visit to the Civil Registry Office on your preferred date and time for marriage-related services.
  • Marriage Certificate Registration:
    - Officially register your marriage to obtain a government-recognized certificate of marriage.
  • Marriage License Registration:
    - Apply for and register your marriage license as a required step before registering your marriage.
  • Request for Marriage Certificate – Certified True Copy (Home Delivery):
    - Request a certified true copy of your marriage certificate and have it delivered to your residence.
  • Delayed Registration of Certificate of Marriage:
    - Submit and process delayed registration if your marriage was not officially registered within the required timeframe.
  • Request for Correction of Marriage Certificate:
    - Request corrections to any clerical or typographical errors on your issued marriage certificate, such as names or dates.

These services are available only for marriages that occurred in the City of Manila. All applications are processed in accordance with local regulations and the guidelines of the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA).

You may initiate your requests online, and receive updates through SMS or email. For in-person follow-ups or submission of original documents, please proceed to the Manila Civil Registry Office.

Need additional assistance? Email us at support@manilacivil.gov.ph or visit the office during business hours.

We are committed to providing fast, accurate, and citizen-friendly services to everyone in Manila. Thank you for using our system.
