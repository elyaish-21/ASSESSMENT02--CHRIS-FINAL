Need Help with Death Services?

Welcome to the Civil Registry Information System of the City of Manila! This platform is dedicated to assisting residents and individuals with civil registration concerns related to deaths within Manila.

Here’s what you can do under Death Services in the City of Manila:

  • Schedule of Appointment:
    - Book a visit to the Civil Registry Office for death-related services at your preferred date and time for a smoother and more efficient process.
  • Death Registration:
    - Officially register a person’s death to obtain a legal death certificate as required by law.
  • Delayed Death Registration:
    - Register a death that was not reported within the required period by submitting supporting documents and affidavits.
  • Request for Death Certificate – Certified True Copy (Home Delivery):
    - Request a certified true copy of a death certificate and have it delivered to your residence for convenience.
  • Correction of Death Certificate:
    - Submit a request to correct any clerical or typographical errors found in an issued death certificate.
  • Burial Permit Application:
    - Apply for an official permit required for the burial or interment of a deceased individual.
  • Cremation Permit Application:
    - Submit the necessary documents to obtain a permit for the cremation of a deceased person.

These services are available only for deaths that occurred in the City of Manila. All applications are processed in accordance with local regulations and PSA guidelines.

You may initiate your requests online, and receive updates through SMS or email. For in-person follow-ups or submission of original documents, please proceed to the Manila Civil Registry Office.

Need additional assistance? Email us at support@manilacivil.gov.ph or visit the office during business hours.

We are committed to providing fast, accurate, and citizen-friendly services to everyone in Manila. Thank you for using our system.
