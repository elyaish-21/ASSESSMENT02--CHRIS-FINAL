📋 Death Certificate Appointments Requirements

✅ General Requirements

  • Death Certificate
  • Valid IDs: Government-issued IDs (e.g., Passport, Driver's License, UMID, PhilHealth ID, Voter's ID)
  • Autopsy Report: Filled out and signed by a forensic pathologist or a medical examiner

📝 Additional Based on Circumstances

  • For Non-Biological Family:
    • Valid ID
    • PSA
    • Autorization Letter: Authorized by the deceased family
