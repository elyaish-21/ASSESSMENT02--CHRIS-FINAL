WHAT DO WE DO?

The Civil Registry Information System (CRIS) is a digital platform dedicated to modernizing how civil records are managed. We focus on supporting local government units by providing an efficient and reliable system for handling vital records such as birth, marriage, and death registrations. It streamlines processes, reduces manual errors, and enhances data accessibility. With CRIS, citizens can experience faster services and improved transparency in civil registry transactions.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to simplify civil documentation processes, reduce paperwork, and promote secure record-keeping in a fast-paced digital environment. CRIS was developed with both accessibility and accountability in mind that will help communities maintain accurate civil records with ease.
